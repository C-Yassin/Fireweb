FlameGet

A blazing fast, lightweight downloader built for speed and reliability. From native browser integration and full torrent support to auto-merging high-quality media, FlameGet handles it all seamlessly.

Download Now View Source

Blazing Fast Speeds

FlameGet uses advanced multi-threading to split your files into smaller pieces, downloading them simultaneously to max out your bandwidth and bring your files home faster.

Auto-Merge Media

Stop dealing with separate video and audio files. FlameGet leverages the power of FFmpeg to automatically stitch high-quality DASH tracks together completely in the background.

Native GTK4 Design

Built with Python and the modern GTK4 toolkit, FlameGet looks and feels like a native application. Enjoy smooth animations, crisp fonts, and full dark mode support. Plus it's fully customizable with CSS!
